    About Us

    The Computer Science Department is at the forefront of innovation in the ever-changing world of technology. Our faculty are experts in their fields and are passionate about teaching and mentoring the next generation of computer scientists.

    Our students are highly motivated and talented, and they go on to successful careers in a variety of industries, including software development, web development, data science, and artificial intelligence.

    Research

    Our faculty are engaged in cutting-edge research in a variety of areas, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, and software engineering.
