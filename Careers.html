    • Careers

    The Computer Science Department offers a wide range of career opportunities for our graduates. Our students are highly sought after by employers in a variety of industries, including software development, web development, data science, and artificial intelligence.

    Career Paths

    • Software Developer
    • Web Developer
    • Data Scientist
    • Machine Learning Engineer
    • Information Security Analyst
    • Network Administrator
    • Database Administrator

    How to Get a Job in Computer Science

    There are a number of things you can do to increase your chances of getting a job in computer science. These include:

    • Get a strong foundation in computer science fundamentals.
    • Develop a strong portfolio of projects.
    • Complete internships or co-ops.
    • Network with professionals in the field.
    • Attend career fairs and workshops.
